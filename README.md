## What this is
 
Four times a year, we gather. Three voices from the cloud-native ecosystem
in DACH, one circle of seven-minute pitches, an honest hour after.
Recorded, kept, carried out into the world.
 
The Roundtables are the community arm of the
[Open Ecosystem Collective](https://open-ecosystem-collective.com),
where the methodology gets faces and the partner landscape gets voices.
 
## At a glance
 
- **Cadence:** Quarterly, four evenings a year
- **Time:** 17:00 to 20:00
- **Venue:** Somewhere in Zurich, the address travels with the RSVP
- **On stage:** Three orgs per evening, the cast turns with the season
- **Recorded:** Every pitch is published, every partner keeps their own clip
- **Format:** Lightning pitches, moderated Q&A, conversation until close
See [`format/agenda.md`](format/agenda.md) for the shape of an evening
minute by minute.

## How partners participate
 
A partner takes a seat at the table by bringing:
 
- A prepared seven-minute pitch that lands
- Time in the moderated Q&A
- Presence for the full 17:00 to 20:00 window
- A guest list of five to eight customers or customer-adjacent people
- A jointly funded share of food, drinks, and merch with the other two
  partners on stage
- Willingness to be recorded and to have the clip released
- Discretion about the venue
**No financial contribution to VRC. No sponsorship fee to the OEC.**
The investment is presence, preparation, hospitality, and the cost of
feeding the room together with two peers around the same fire.
 
See [`format/partner-brief.md`](format/partner-brief.md) for the full
partner brief, and [`format/faq.md`](format/faq.md) for the questions
that keep coming up.
 
## What we are trying to do
 
1. **Raise the level** by making the craft of partner management visible
2. **Give smaller cloud-native companies a stage** they would not otherwise get
3. **Build a place where the ecosystem can see itself**, four times a year
4. **Leave behind an honest archive** of unguarded partnership conversations
## The arrangement
 
Three roles carry the evening:
 
- **Organisation** by [Vonarburg-Romero Consulting](https://vonarburg-romero-consulting.com)
- **Venue** offered by a quiet host in Zurich
- **Food, drinks, merch** jointly sponsored by the three voices on stage
No hidden sponsors. No pay-to-stage. No membership tiers.
If anything ever changes, it will change visibly, in writing, here.

## Support the work
 
The Roundtables are free to attend. Partners on stage cover the food
and drinks for the evening. VRC carries the rest: the moderation, the
recordings, the editing, the meetup logistics, the website, the
governance, the slow work of building this into something the whole
ecosystem can rely on.
 
If the Roundtables, the methodology, or the wider Open Ecosystem
Collective have been useful to you and you want to help the work
keep growing, donations to VRC are gratefully received. They go
toward building the business behind the Collective and toward
professionalising the Open Ecosystem - the kind of work that makes
the next edition, the next playbook, the next set of recordings
possible.
 
What donations do **not** buy:
 
- A spot on stage
- Influence over the partner cast
- A logo, a mention, or any other visible recognition
- A different conversation than anyone else gets
What they support:
 
- Keeping the Roundtables free for attendees
- Keeping the methodology open under CC-BY-SA 4.0
- Time to do the work between editions: editing, writing,
  reaching out, growing the format
- Building the Open Ecosystem Collective into a lasting reference
  for partner ecosystem management in the cloud-native space
Reach out through [Vonarburg-Romero Consulting](https://vonarburg-romero-consulting.com)
if you want to support the work. Tania will respond personally and
explain what is possible from there.
